// About EMS-IOT

Rooted in Shenzhen's electronics ecosystem. 15 years of manufacturing IoT modules for global partners.

The Shenzhen Advantage, Engineered Into Every Module

Founded in 2010 in the heart of Shenzhen's Bao'an electronics corridor, EMS-IOT began as a small PCB assembly shop serving Huaqiangbei trading firms. Over 15 years, we have grown into a 100,000-square-foot dedicated IoT module manufacturing facility with 12 high-speed SMT lines running 24/6.

Our facility sits 15 minutes from SEG Electronics Market, 20 minutes from Shenzhen Bao'an Airport, and 40 minutes from Yantian Container Port. This hyper-proximity to the world's densest component supply chain gives our customers an unmatchable advantage: same-day BOM quoting, next-day prototype assembly, and aggressive unit economics that no factory outside the Pearl River Delta can match.

Today, we ship over 5 million modules annually to customers across North America, Europe, Southeast Asia, and the Middle East. Our engineering team of 40+ RF, embedded, and quality specialists provides end-to-end NPI support from schematic review to mass production.
